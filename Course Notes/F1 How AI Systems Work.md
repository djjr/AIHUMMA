The is the most important course in the program to design well because it lays the technical foundation that most of our students will not bring to the program and because it proclaims how the program conveys technical competence without just trying to teach students to code. The goal is _functional literacy_, not technical competency. The goal is not "you should be able to apply AI to your research"; it is "you should understand how AI works enough to be able to think critically and engage with experts about it."
