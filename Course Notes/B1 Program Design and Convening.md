**3 credits | Track B: Operations, Programs, and Communications**
##### Description
How do you design a fellowship? A workshop? A convening that produces something? This course treats program design as an intellectual craft, drawing on literatures from organizational sociology, adult learning theory, and the history of influential knowledge-building institutions (the Asilomar conference, the Santa Fe Institute, Pugwash). Students design a real program — a fellowship selection process, a three-day workshop curriculum, a field-building initiative — and defend it.
